# Usage Example

[中文](./usage.md)

Below is a complete example of how to place the declaration at the beginning of an article.

## Markdown Example

```md
> AI Content Transparency
> AI Ratio: 35%
> Human Ratio: 65%
> AI Roles: outline drafting, language polishing, title suggestions
> Human Roles: topic selection, argument design, fact review, final approval
> Note: This declaration is voluntarily provided by the author to explain AI participation in this article.

# Why Content Creation Needs AI Transparency

The main body starts here...
```

## HTML Example

```html
<section class="ai-disclosure">
  <strong>AI Content Transparency</strong><br>
  AI Ratio: 35%<br>
  Human Ratio: 65%<br>
  AI Roles: outline drafting, language polishing, title suggestions<br>
  Human Roles: topic selection, argument design, fact review, final approval
</section>
```

## Notes

- Place the declaration before the main body
- Keep the wording specific
- Do not use the declaration as a marketing slogan or misleading trust badge
